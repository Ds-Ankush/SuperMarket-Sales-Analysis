# 📊 Business Insights

## 🏢 Branch Analysis

Giza is the best performing Branch Overall. Giza Perform Best in Both Revenue and customer Satisfaction. Cairo is the weakest branch in terms of revenue and rating. Alex sells the most products but generate less revenue from Giza and More from Cairo. Giza is doing differently and the Company should replicate those practices in other branches.

---

## 🌍 City Analysis

Naypyitaw is the best performing City across all major metrics. It Generate highest Revenue, highest Average Transaction and Highest Customer Ratings. Other Side Yangon and Mandalay Perform Similar In terms of revenue contribution. Supermarket should investigate the factors driving Naypyitaw's superior performance and replicate successful strategies in other cities.

---

## 🛍️ Product Line Analysis

Food And Beverages is the Top performing Product Line. Food And Beverages Generating highest total revenue and Receiving highest Average customer ratings. In Gross Income Fashion Accessories perform better than Food And Beverages but both Product Lines have no huge difference. Highest Quantity Sold by Electronic Accessories indicates highest product demand but in revenue contribution it holds 3rd Rank and Health And Beauty shows Lowest Revenue Contribution. Overall the business should focus on strengthening high performing categories while improving the performance of weaker segments.

---

## 👥 Customer Type Analysis

Member customers consistently outperformed Normal customers across key business metrics, including revenue, gross income and overall contribution to business performance. This indicates Member customers are more engaged, spend more per transaction and generate higher profitability for the company. In contrast, Normal customers showed lower revenue and profit contributions. The company should try to convert Normal customers into Members for higher profitability and revenue generation.

---

## 🚻 Gender Analysis

Female and Male customers represent **57.1%** and **42.9%** respectively. Female customers contribute more than males and their ratings are also slightly better, indicating high satisfaction with products. Both genders give similarly high ratings. Female customers mostly prefer Fashion Accessories, while Male customers prefer Food And Beverages and Electronic Accessories equally. Overall, Female customers are the primary drivers of Sales and Business Growth.

---

## 💳 Payment Method Analysis

E-wallet is the most frequently used payment method, indicating its popularity among customers for everyday transactions. However, Cash generates the highest total revenue and the highest average transaction value, suggesting that Cash users tend to make larger purchases. Member customers primarily prefer Cash payments, while Normal customers mostly use E-wallet. These findings indicate that payment preferences vary by customer type, providing an opportunity for the business to design targeted payment-based promotions and loyalty offers.

---

## ⏰ Time-Based Analysis

January generated the highest total revenue, while February recorded the lowest, indicating stronger customer spending at the beginning of the year. Among weekdays, Saturday was the highest revenue-generating day, whereas Monday recorded the lowest sales. Customer activity peaked at **7:00 PM (19:00)**, which also recorded the highest number of transactions. Throughout the day, sales fluctuated during business hours, then increased sharply after **6:00 PM**, reaching their peak around **7:00 PM** before declining toward the store's closing time at **8:00 PM**. This suggests that the evening is the most critical sales period, making it an ideal time to ensure adequate staffing, inventory availability and promotional activities.

---

## 💰 Tax & Profit Analysis

The supermarket collected a total tax of **15,379.37**, with an average tax of approximately **15.38** per transaction, indicating a consistent tax contribution across purchases. Giza Branch generated the highest gross income among all branches, while Naypyitaw was the most profitable city. Among product categories, Food And Beverages contributed the highest gross income. The remaining branches and cities generated relatively similar gross income, reflecting a balanced business performance across most locations.

---

## ⭐ Rating Analysis

The supermarket maintains a strong customer satisfaction level with an average rating of approximately **7 out of 10**. All branches and cities have achieved a maximum customer rating of **10**, indicating consistent service quality across locations. Among product categories, Electronic Accessories and Health & Beauty received the highest perfect rating of **10**, while the remaining product lines achieved ratings close to **9.9**, reflecting high customer satisfaction across all categories. Additionally, higher sales transactions do not receive higher ratings, suggesting that customer satisfaction depends more on the overall shopping experience and product quality than on the transaction value.

---

## 📈 Correlation Analysis

When Sales increase, Gross Income increases directly, showing a very strong positive correlation. Quantity has a strong positive influence on Sales, indicating that transactions with larger purchase quantities tend to generate higher revenue. Unit Price also positively influences Sales. Tax, Sales and COGS are highly correlated with each other, meaning that as Sales increase, Tax and COGS also increase. Overall, the company should focus on maintaining sufficient stock levels for high-demand products, as higher quantities sold directly contribute to increased revenue.
